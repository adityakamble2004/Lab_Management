About Us

Welcome to our Student Complaint Portal! Our platform is dedicated to streamlining the process of reporting and resolving issues in the college computer labs. We aim to improve the overall experience for students by ensuring that technical problems are addressed efficiently and effectively.

This portal allows students to log their issues related to hardware, software, or network problems and track their resolution status. We believe in creating a user-friendly and transparent system for handling complaints.
