About Our Website

The Computer Lab Complaint Portal is designed to streamline the process of reporting and resolving issues in the lab. Below are some key features of the portal:

  • Simple and user-friendly interface for reporting issues.
  • Search functionality to look up previously reported issues.
  • Secure login and sign-up system for personalized access.
  • Real-time updates on the status of your reported issues.
  • Admin dashboard for managing and resolving complaints efficiently.

Website Structure

The portal consists of the following sections:

  • Home Page: An overview of the portal with quick access to key features.
  • Report Issue Page: A form for users to submit their complaints.
  • Issue Tracker: A section for users to view the status of their reported issues.
  • Admin Dashboard: For administrators to manage complaints and provide resolutions.
  • Contact Us: A page to get in touch with the support team.

Future Enhancements

We plan to add more features, including:

  • Real-time notifications for updates on reported issues.
  • Integration with mobile platforms for easier access.
  • Detailed analytics for administrators to track common issues.
